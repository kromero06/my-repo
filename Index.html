About Me

My name is Karla Romero. I grew up in Bethesda, I like creating art, and I personally think I have a good sense of humor. I've been trying to read more, but I spend a lot of my time studying at the library or scrolling on Rednote discovering new styles and artists. Traveling is something I really love, I even wated to be a pilot/flight attendent when I was younger. My favorite vacation spot is Yanga, Veracruz

My favorite vacation spot is Yanga, Veracruz located in Mexico, specifically my parents hometown. One of the main reasons I love going there is because of the annual festival that celebrates and honors Gaspar Yanga. The culture, history, and love in our community always make me feel so attached to this town. I often find myself thinking about what my family is up to because I miss and love them. Most of my favorite memories are from Mexico, I always have a different experiance when I go back, My dad said he'll buy the family a quad bike so I'm excited for that since almost every family has one over there. The food, music, and traditions make it a place that will always feel special to me.
